# Privacy Skills

Open-source agent skills for checking privacy risks before sensitive data is uploaded, shared, logged, automated, or used with AI.

These skills help people ask better privacy questions across everyday work, team governance, and software architecture. They are practical review tools, not legal advice.

## Everyday privacy skills

Useful for anyone handling documents, photos, messages, or meetings.

- [Before You Upload Check](../skills/before-you-upload-check/README.md) — check anything before uploading it to an AI tool or external service
- [Screenshot Privacy Check](../skills/screenshot-privacy-check/README.md) — review screenshots for personal data before sharing
- [Form Privacy Check](../skills/form-privacy-check/README.md) — check forms for unnecessary or risky data collection
- [Safe Sharing Version](../skills/safe-sharing-version/README.md) — rewrite content into a safer version for sharing
- [Minimum Data Needed](../skills/minimum-data-needed/README.md) — decide the least amount of data needed for a task
- [AI Upload Decision Check](../skills/ai-upload-decision-check/README.md) — decide whether content is safe to upload to an AI tool
- [Meeting Recording Privacy Check](../skills/meeting-recording-privacy-check/README.md) — assess the privacy risks of recording a meeting
- [Public Case Study Sanitiser](../skills/public-case-study-sanitiser/README.md) — convert internal examples into safe public content
- [Email Privacy Check](../skills/email-privacy-check/README.md) — review emails before sending for oversharing risks
- [LinkedIn Post Privacy Check](../skills/linkedin-post-privacy-check/README.md) — check posts before publishing on LinkedIn
- [Cloud Storage Sharing Check](../skills/cloud-storage-sharing-check/README.md) — review cloud file sharing settings
- [AI Meeting Notes Check](../skills/ai-meeting-notes-check/README.md) — check AI-generated meeting notes for privacy risks
- [Photo Metadata Privacy Check](../skills/photo-metadata-privacy-check/README.md) — check photos for hidden metadata and identifying details
