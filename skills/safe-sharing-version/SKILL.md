# Safe Sharing Version

## Quick start

Transform content into a safer version for sharing.

Preserve the useful meaning while reducing unnecessary exposure.

## What to remove or generalise

- Real names
- Exact locations
- Company names unless approved
- Customer details
- Student or child details
- Internal project names
- Account numbers
- Identifying dates
- Private screenshots
- Unique combinations of facts
- Confidential business details

## Workflow

1. Identify the sharing destination.
2. Identify sensitive details.
3. Decide what can be removed, generalised, replaced with synthetic examples, aggregated, or kept.
4. Rewrite the content.
5. Explain the changes briefly.

## Output format

Return:

- Privacy issues found
- Safer rewritten version
- Notes on what was changed
- Remaining risks if any

## Disclaimer

This is a practical privacy review tool, not legal advice.
